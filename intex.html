N.T. Rama Rao Jr., often known as Jr. NTR, is a prominent Indian actor, Kuchipudi dancer, and producer who primarily works in Telugu cinema. He is the grandson of the legendary actor and former Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, N.T. Rama Rao. Jr. NTR has established himself as one of the leading actors in Tollywood, known for his action-oriented roles and powerful performances. Early Life and Family: Born on May 20, 1983, in Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh (now Telangana), to Nandamuri Harikrishna and Shalini Bhaskar Rao. His father, Nandamuri Harikrishna, was also a Telugu film actor and politician. He is the grandson of the iconic N.T. Rama Rao, a Telugu cinema legend. He has a half-brother, Nandamuri Kalyan Ram, who is also an actor and producer. His wife is Narne Lakshmi Pranathi, and they have two sons, Nandamuri Abhay Ram and Nandamuri Bhargava Ram. Career: Jr. NTR's acting debut was as a child artist in the movie "Ramayanam" (1996). He transitioned to lead roles with "Ninnu Choodalani" (2001), but his breakthrough came with "Student No. 1" (2001). He has starred in numerous successful films, including "Aadi" (2002), "Simhadri" (2003), "Rakhi" (2006), "Yamadonga" (2007), "Brindavanam" (2010), "Temper" (2015), "Nannaku Prematho" (2016), "Janatha Garage" (2016), "Jai Lava Kusa" (2017), and "Aravinda Sametha Veera Raghava" (2018). He is known for his intense action sequences and captivating screen presence. He has received several accolades, including two Filmfare Awards, two Nandi Awards, and four CineMAA Awards.
